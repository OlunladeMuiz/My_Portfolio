Dental Care App

This was a project I built early in 2019 when I first learned javascript. I was the sole creator/developer for the first year of the project before I was able to hire help.

The aim of this app was to assist in dental issues such as toothache and so on, How it can reduce the cases of tooth issues amongst us.

Customers had the ability to post different types of issue they had regarding their teeth and then get an appointment through the contact us page

I worked on this projcet for over a month, making sure every functionality was intact and had no issues
